K.M. Patel Balshala Shishuvihar - Nursery

Managed by Charotar Education Society is located in Mahatma Gandhi Vidyalaya Campus in Mogri. The school was established in the year 1997.

When the school was established, the first principal of that time was Shri PannaBen Patel. and currently Mafatlal Prajapati is the Principal. More than 200 students are studying in the school. Activities related to various cultural studies,games are carried out in the school for the overall development of the students.
The full name of the school is Kamlaben Manibhai Patel.

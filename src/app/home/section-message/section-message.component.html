Message From From Principal's desk

Mrs Ratna Gurjar
- Principal, K.M. Patel Shishuvihar / Balshala

It is rightly said that, just as a strong and enduring structure requires a solid foundation, the same principle applies to nurturing our most precious national assets – our future, i.e. our children.

Raising confident children is one of the most difficult responsibilities for both parents and teachers. It's rightly said that parents serve as the child's first educators, while teachers take on the role of their second parents. Thus, it is essential for both to come together in the growth and development of the child.

In addition to the home environment, I strongly believe that the school should offer a nurturing space for children one that encourages curiosity as the initial step towards learning social skills and instilling values.

In our school, we uphold the belief that every student has the potential to succeed and reach their full potential. We are dedicated to creating a safe and supportive atmosphere where students can learn and flourish. Our mission is to ensure that they not only learn to communicate effectively and explore their surroundings but also internalize values that will guide them in their journey towards positive growth.

So, on behalf of the school, I appeal to all the stakeholders to come together to make our children’s first schooling experience a memorable one and set them on the path of lifelong learning to become responsible and skilled citizens that shape the destiny of this great country.
